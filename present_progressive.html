Task 1: Circle the correct answer.

משימה 1: הקיפו את התשובה הנכונה.

1. My friends ________ football in the park now.

a) am playing b) is playing c) are playing

2. Look! The cat ________ on the tree.

a) am climbing b) is climbing c) are climbing

3. I ________ my homework at the moment.

a) am doing b) is doing c) are doing

4. Dana and I ________ a delicious cake.

a) am baking b) is baking c) are baking

5. Listen! The teacher ________ to us.

a) am speaking b) is speaking c) are speaking

Task 2: Match the English sentence to its Hebrew translation.

משימה 2: התאימו בין המשפט באנגלית לתרגומו הנכון בעברית.

3. They are singing a song. (___)

ד. הם שרים שיר.
